📚 Thân Thế & Lai Lịch

Sóng Vô Tuyến Đại Sư bắt đầu với Wi-Fi B/G — 11 Mbps shared, toàn bộ tầng 3 văn phòng dùng 1 access point. "Tại sao mạng chậm?" — "Vì 100 người chia 11 Mbps, 0.11 Mbps mỗi người." Đó là những ngày đau khổ.

Chàng ta chứng kiến evolution: 802.11a/b/g → n (MIMO!) → ac (5GHz!) → ax/Wi-Fi 6 (OFDMA!) → be/Wi-Fi 7 (320MHz channels!). Mỗi thế hệ hứa hẹn "finally fix Wi-Fi" — và mỗi thế hệ tạo ra expectations mới.

Rồi 5G xuất hiện với lời hứa "1Gbps everywhere." Đại Sư cười: "1Gbps với mmWave chỉ hoạt động khi bạn đứng cạnh antenna, không có mưa, không có cây, và không có tường. Tức là chỉ trong lab."

📜 Bí Kíp

1. Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) — Key Features: OFDMA: chia channel thành sub-channels, serve nhiều clients simultaneously. MU-MIMO: up to 8 spatial streams (vs 4 in ac). Target Wake Time (TWT): tiết kiệm pin cho IoT devices. BSS Coloring: reduce interference giữa neighboring APs. 1024-QAM: higher data rates. Cần Wi-Fi 6 clients để tận dụng — legacy clients vẫn hoạt động nhưng không benefit.
2. Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be) — Next Gen: 320 MHz channels (gấp đôi Wi-Fi 6). 4096-QAM. Multi-Link Operation (MLO): aggregate 2.4 + 5 + 6 GHz simultaneously. 16 MU-MIMO spatial streams. Preamble puncturing cho flexible spectrum use. Target: 46 Gbps max throughput. Availability: 2024-2026. NHƯNG: cần 6 GHz spectrum (Wi-Fi 6E/7).
3. Enterprise WLAN Design: Site survey: measure RF propagation, identify coverage gaps, interference. AP placement: coverage overlap 20-30% cho roaming. Channel planning: avoid co-channel interference. Power tuning: don't max out — causes interference. Band steering: push capable clients to 5/6 GHz. QoS: prioritize voice/video traffic. Minimum 1 AP per 25-30 users cho good experience.
4. 5G — Reality vs Marketing: Sub-6 GHz: decent coverage, 100-400 Mbps typical. mmWave: 1+ Gbps but 200m range, blocked by walls/rain. Mid-band (C-band): sweet spot — coverage + speed. Network slicing: dedicated virtual network per use case. Private 5G: enterprise deployments for manufacturing/logistics. Real-world 5G ≠ demo 5G.
5. Security — WPA3: WPA3-Personal: SAE (Simultaneous Authentication of Equals), better than PSK. WPA3-Enterprise: 192-bit security suite, certificate-based. Enhanced Open (OWE): encrypted Wi-Fi without password (guest networks). Disable WPA2 khi possible. 802.1X cho enterprise — individual credentials per user. MAC filtering: NOT security (MAC spoofing trivial).
6. Troubleshooting Wireless: Signal strength (RSSI): -65 dBm minimum cho reliable connection. SNR: 25+ dB for good quality. Channel utilization: under 50% for good performance. Client roaming issues: check 802.11r/k/v support. Spectrum analyzer cho interference hunting. Wireshark in monitor mode cho packet-level debugging.

📖 Chuyện Kể Đêm Khuya

📡 Chuyện Wi-Fi 6 AP Can Thiệp Radar

Deploy Wi-Fi 6 APs ở tòa nhà gần sân bay. 5 GHz DFS channels. AP detect radar signal → Dynamic Frequency Selection (DFS) switch channel → all clients disconnect momentarily → reconnect → detect radar again → switch again. Loop. Users experience intermittent dropouts every few minutes.

Root cause: radar từ sân bay trigger DFS liên tục trên DFS channels. Fix: switch to non-DFS channels (36, 40, 44, 48) — fewer channels nhưng no radar interference. Hoặc deploy Wi-Fi 6E (6 GHz band — no DFS required).

💡 Bài học: DFS channels (52-144 ở 5GHz) có thể bị radar interference. Near airports, military bases, weather stations — avoid DFS channels. Non-DFS channels ít hơn nhưng reliable hơn. Wi-Fi 6E/7 ở 6 GHz band = no DFS = no radar issues.

📶 Chuyện 5G Coverage Gap

Carrier promises 5G coverage cho warehouse 10,000m². Deploy 5G Private Network với mmWave. Demo tuyệt vời: 2 Gbps throughput! Production: forklifts, metal shelves, moving objects → signal blocked liên tục. Coverage gaps everywhere. IoT sensors lose connectivity, tracking system unreliable.

Fix: replace mmWave với mid-band (n78/n77). Lower throughput (500 Mbps) nhưng better penetration. Add more small cells cho coverage. Result: 99.5% coverage reliability. Lesson: demo environment ≠ production environment.

💡 Bài học: mmWave: amazing speed, terrible penetration. Metal, walls, even people block signal. For industrial/warehouse: mid-band 5G hoặc Wi-Fi 6. Site survey trong realistic conditions (with machinery, people, inventory). Coverage > throughput cho IoT use cases.

🏢 Chuyện Conference Room Wi-Fi Nightmare

Meeting room cho 50 người. 1 AP overhead. Works fine cho 5 people. All-hands meeting 50 people: everyone opens laptop, phone → 100+ devices on 1 AP. Bandwidth per device: 2 Mbps. Zoom calls buffering. Presentations not loading. CEO frustrated.

💡 Bài học: High-density areas (conference rooms, auditoriums) need dedicated APs. 1 AP per 25-30 clients. Band steering push clients to 5/6 GHz. OFDMA helps but AP capacity is still finite. Pre-event testing cho large meetings.

⚠️ Những Sai Lầm Kinh Điển

1. Một AP cho cả tầng: Coverage ≠ Capacity. 1 AP may cover whole floor nhưng 100 clients = terrible performance. Plan AP density based on user count.
2. Max TX power: Tăng power = tăng interference + asymmetric connection (AP hears client nhưng client too far to respond). Proper power tuning > max power.
3. WPA2/PSK cho enterprise: Shared password = no accountability. 802.1X with individual credentials. WPA2/PSK chỉ cho guest/IoT.
4. Ignore 2.4 GHz planning: 'Ai còn dùng 2.4 GHz?' IoT devices, old laptops. 2.4 GHz channels: chỉ 1, 6, 11 non-overlapping. Plan carefully.
5. No guest network isolation: Guest on same VLAN as corporate. Guest device compromised → access internal resources. Separate VLAN, firewall rules cho guest network.
