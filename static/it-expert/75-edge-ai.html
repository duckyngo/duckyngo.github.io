📜 Bí Kíp Edge AI

1

Model Compression — Nhỏ hơn, nhanh hơn, rẻ hơn

4 techniques: (1) Quantization: FP32 → INT8 → INT4 (4-8x smaller). (2) Pruning: remove unnecessary weights/neurons (2-10x smaller). (3) Knowledge Distillation: train small model mimic large model. (4) Architecture Design: MobileNet, EfficientNet — designed for edge. Combine: distill + prune + quantize = 50-100x smaller. Workflow: train full model → distill → prune → quantize → deploy.

2

Quantization cho Edge

Post-training quantization (PTQ): quantize sau training, dễ nhưng quality loss. Quantization-aware training (QAT): simulate quantization during training, better quality. INT8: standard cho edge GPU (Jetson, phone). INT4: microcontrollers. TFLite: converter.optimizations = [tf.lite.Optimize.DEFAULT]. TensorRT: INT8 calibration tự động. ONNX Runtime: INT8/INT4 support. Luôn compare quantized vs original trên your eval set.

3

Knowledge Distillation — Teacher-Student

Large model (teacher) → train small model (student) to mimic teacher outputs. Student learns "soft targets" (probability distributions) thay vì hard labels → richer learning signal. Temperature: T=3-5 soften teacher outputs. Loss: α × distillation_loss + (1-α) × student_loss, α ≈ 0.7. Results: student 3-10x smaller, retain 90-98% teacher performance. Dùng cho: deploy BERT on mobile, deploy YOLO on Jetson.

4

Edge Hardware Landscape

NVIDIA Jetson (Orin Nano/AGX): GPU-accelerated, TensorRT, best cho vision. Raspberry Pi 5: versatile, cheap, good cho prototyping. ESP32: $3 microcontroller, TinyML, keyword detection. Coral (Google): Edge TPU, fast inference, limited model support. Apple Neural Engine: Core ML, on-device LLM. Qualcomm AI Hub: mobile AI. Choose: compute budget → hardware → model compression target.

5

TinyML — AI trên microcontroller

Constraints: 256KB RAM, 1MB Flash, no OS, no floating point. Tools: TensorFlow Lite Micro, Edge Impulse (no-code TinyML platform). Models: keyword spotting (2-3 keywords), gesture recognition, anomaly detection (vibration, sound). Trick: feature engineering quan trọng hơn model complexity ở edge. Mel spectrogram cho audio, simple CNN cho images. Target: < 100ms inference, < 100KB model.

📖 Chuyện Kể Đêm Khuya

📦 Chuyện 1: Fitting model vào 256KB

IoT device cần detect 3 loại tiếng (glass break, smoke alarm, baby cry). RAM: 256KB. Flash: 1MB. Anh Vi Tiểu Mô bắt đầu với model 5MB: accuracy 96%. Prune: 2MB, 94%. Quantize INT8: 500KB, 93%. Vẫn quá lớn.

Redesign architecture: 3 depthwise separable conv layers, 1 dense layer. Params: 15K. Size: 60KB. Accuracy: 88%. Đủ tốt? Client: "Detect thêm 2 loại tiếng nữa." Anh: "... 256KB vẫn là 256KB."

💡 Bài học: Edge AI = constant battle against constraints. Process: start với accuracy target → design lightest model that meets it → compress further. Don't start với large model rồi compress — design cho edge từ đầu. 88% accuracy on device > 96% accuracy cần cloud.
🤖 Chuyện 2: Inference on Arduino — art of impossible

Intern hỏi: "Chạy image classification trên Arduino Uno (2KB RAM) được không?" Anh: "Không." Intern: "Challenge accepted."

3 tuần sau, intern demo: 8x8 pixel grayscale image → 3-class classifier (circle, square, triangle). Model: 5 neurons, 200 bytes. Accuracy: 91%. Arduino Uno inference: 2ms. "Technically, đó là image classification trên Arduino," anh acknowledge. "Nhưng 8x8 pixels thì... hơi thách thức cho real-world applications."

💡 Bài học: TinyML possible nhưng requires extreme constraints acceptance. Match expectations với hardware: Arduino = simple patterns (audio keywords, vibration). Jetson = real vision tasks. Don't try to fit elephant vào matchbox — chọn đúng hardware cho task.
⚡ Chuyện 3: TensorRT tăng tốc 10x — nhưng...

YOLO trên Jetson Orin: PyTorch = 8 FPS. Export ONNX: 15 FPS. TensorRT FP16: 45 FPS. TensorRT INT8: 85 FPS. 10x speedup! Deploy production.

1 tuần sau: customer report model không detect objects nhỏ. Investigation: INT8 quantization lost precision cho small objects. TensorRT calibration data không include small objects sufficiently. Fix: better calibration dataset + mixed precision (INT8 backbone, FP16 detection head). Result: 65 FPS, detect small objects correctly.

💡 Bài học: Quantization speedup impressive nhưng LUÔN validate quality trên YOUR test set, đặc biệt edge cases (small objects, rare classes). Calibration data phải representative. Mixed precision (some layers FP16, some INT8) cho best accuracy-speed tradeoff.

⚠️ Những Sai Lầm Kinh Điển

❌ Train large model rồi mới nghĩ đến edge: Design cho edge từ đầu (MobileNet, EfficientNet-Lite). Compress 500MB → 500KB hiếm khi work.
❌ Chỉ test accuracy, bỏ qua latency: 95% accuracy nhưng 500ms inference = useless cho real-time. Measure both.
❌ Quantize mà không calibrate: INT8 cần calibration data. Random calibration → bad quantization → bad accuracy.
❌ Ignore power consumption: Battery device chạy GPU model = pin hết trong 1 giờ. Measure power/inference.
❌ Prototype trên PC, deploy trên edge mà không benchmark: "Chạy tốt trên laptop" ≠ "chạy được trên Raspberry Pi."
